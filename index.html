Vitiligo is a condition where the skin loses its pigment, resulting in white patches that can appear anywhere on the body. It's crucial to raise awareness about vitiligo to dispel myths and promote understanding. While there is no known way to prevent vitiligo, individuals can take certain precautions to manage the condition if it occurs.
